<h1 align="center">OSINT JOURNO</h1>

<p align="center">
  Open-source intelligence for investigative journalism
</p>

---

**OSINT JOURNO** is an open-source intelligence (OSINT) organization dedicated to supporting investigative journalism, digital research, and ethical intelligence gathering.  
We develop and share tools, frameworks, and research methodologies that help journalists, researchers, and analysts uncover, verify, and analyze publicly available information in the public interest.
